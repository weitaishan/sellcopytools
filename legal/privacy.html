Privacy Policy

SellCopy Tools is a free browser-based ecommerce copy generator. The site does not require an account and does not ask you to submit payment information.

Information you provide

When you use the generator, you may enter product names, features, audiences, tones, platforms, and benefits. The current version processes those inputs in your browser to generate copy drafts.

Analytics

SellCopy Tools uses Google Analytics to understand site usage, traffic sources, popular pages, and basic engagement. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect aggregated usage data.

Advertising and affiliate links

The site may add display advertising or affiliate links in the future. Those services may use cookies or tracking technologies according to their own policies.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to contact@sellcopytools.com.
