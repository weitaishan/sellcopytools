What product captions should do

A product caption should give context to the visual. It can explain the benefit, show a use case, tell a short story, or invite the audience to take the next step. The best captions do not repeat what the image already says; they add a reason to care.

  • Start with a specific hook.
  • Connect the product to a real use case.
  • Keep the call to action simple.
  • Match the tone to your brand and audience.
  • Use hashtags intentionally instead of stuffing them.

Caption angles to try

Try launch announcement, problem-solution, customer favorite, founder note, gift guide, seasonal routine, styling tip, or “how to use it” angles. Save your strongest angles and reuse them across future posts.

Editing tip

After generating a caption, add one detail from the actual product photo or campaign. Specific details make captions feel less generic and more connected to the brand.
