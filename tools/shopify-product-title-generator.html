How Shopify product titles work

Shopify titles appear in product pages, collection grids, search results, browser tabs, and sometimes social previews. A good title should help both shoppers and search engines understand the product quickly.

  • Start with the product type or category.
  • Add one useful differentiator such as material, use case, size, or audience.
  • Keep brand language natural and readable.
  • Avoid stuffing repeated keywords.
  • Make sure the title matches the actual product page content.

Title examples

Instead of “Desk Lamp,” a more useful title might be “Adjustable LED Desk Lamp with Wireless Charging.” Instead of “Travel Bottle,” try “Leakproof Travel Mug for Hot Coffee.” Specific titles give shoppers more context before they click.

Editing tip

Generate several options, then choose the title that is accurate and scannable. If your product page has a separate SEO title field, you can use a slightly more search-focused version there.
