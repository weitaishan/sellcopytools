What is a product value proposition?

A value proposition is a clear statement of the product's audience, outcome, and difference. It helps shoppers understand why the product is relevant to them before they compare price, features, or alternatives.

  • Name the audience or use case.
  • Describe the main outcome or benefit.
  • Support the promise with product details.
  • Explain the difference without overclaiming.
  • Keep the statement easy to repeat.

Useful formula

Try: “For [audience] who need [outcome], [product] helps [benefit] with [key differentiator].” This formula is simple, but it forces the most important strategic choices into one sentence.

Where to use it

Use value proposition drafts in hero sections, product descriptions, paid ad briefs, email launches, sales pages, and team positioning documents. The final version should guide the rest of the page copy.
