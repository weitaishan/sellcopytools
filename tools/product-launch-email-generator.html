What a product launch email should include

A launch email should quickly explain the product, the reason it exists, the customer benefit, and the next step. The best launch copy is specific enough to create interest but concise enough for mobile readers.

  • Open with the new product or collection name.
  • Explain the main benefit in plain language.
  • Highlight 2-4 specific features or use cases.
  • Add a clear call to action.
  • Make launch timing, availability, or offer details accurate.

Email angles to test

Try a founder note, problem-solution announcement, early access message, customer-requested feature angle, seasonal drop, or limited restock. Each angle should still lead back to the product value.

Before sending

Review subject line, preview text, product links, discount rules, inventory status, and image alignment. A good launch email feels coordinated with the product page and the rest of the campaign.
