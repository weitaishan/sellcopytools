What to include in an abandoned cart email

The goal is to remind the shopper what they were interested in and make returning easy. Keep the tone helpful, not desperate. The copy should reduce hesitation and restate the product value.

  • Use a clear subject line.
  • Remind the shopper what is waiting.
  • Repeat one or two product benefits.
  • Include a direct return-to-cart call to action.
  • Mention shipping, guarantee, or support details when they reduce friction.

Email sequence ideas

A simple sequence can include a first reminder, a benefit-focused follow-up, and a final message with urgency or support. Discounts can work, but they should not be the only reason to return.

Editing tip

Personalize the draft with the product name, product image, actual cart link, shipping details, and brand voice. Make sure any discount or urgency is accurate before sending.
