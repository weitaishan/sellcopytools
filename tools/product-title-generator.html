What makes a strong product title?

A strong title is clear before it is clever. It should usually include the product type, the key differentiator, and one useful detail such as size, material, audience, or use case. Different platforms have different title styles, so adjust the final title before publishing.

  • Put the product type near the beginning.
  • Add one meaningful differentiator.
  • Avoid keyword stuffing and repeated phrases.
  • Keep marketplace rules in mind.
  • Make sure the title matches the actual product.

Title formulas

Try formulas like “Product Type for Audience,” “Material Product Type with Benefit,” “Product Type for Use Case,” or “Product Type, Size, Key Feature.” The best formula depends on whether the page is for search, ads, marketplaces, or a brand store.

When to generate more title ideas

Generate several options when launching a new product, refreshing a low-performing listing, creating ad variations, or testing SEO page titles. Choose the version that is accurate, specific, and easy to scan.
