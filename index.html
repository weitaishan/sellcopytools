FAQ

Is this tool free?

Yes. The generator is free to use and designed for ecommerce founders, marketers, and creators.

Can I use the generated copy commercially?

Yes, but you should review and edit the output so it accurately represents your product and claims.

How does this site make money?

The site can earn revenue from display ads, affiliate links, sponsored placements, and future paid features.

What can the sales copy generator create?

It can draft product descriptions, feature bullets, ad hooks, SEO titles, meta descriptions, email blurbs, Etsy tags, Amazon title ideas, and Shopify-ready snippets depending on the platform you choose.

Which platforms does it support?

The generator currently supports Shopify, Amazon, Etsy, Facebook Ads, and email workflows. Each platform returns a different set of copy fields so the output is easier to edit and publish.

Is the generated sales copy ready to publish?

Use the output as a strong first draft. Before publishing, check product accuracy, claims, dimensions, materials, shipping details, and any platform-specific rules for your listing.
